# 08. Automation Benefits And Troubleshooting

## What Automation Saves

- Staff do not manually copy website requests into a spreadsheet.
- Staff get alerts when customers need attention.
- Customer conversations stay linked to the contact.
- Follow-up tasks are created automatically.
- Failed sends are visible instead of silently lost.

## Common Issues

- **No customer SMS arrived:** check SMS automation health and Twilio trial recipient rules.
- **No staff alert:** check notification preferences and verified business phone.
- **Duplicate contact:** open Duplicates and merge carefully.
- **Missing reservation details:** use Inbox to ask for the missing date, time, or party size.
