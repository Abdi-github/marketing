# 06. Deals, Segments, And Returning Customers

## Business Goal

Some leads are simple reservations. Others are bigger opportunities. The CRM should help staff separate daily requests from high-value business.

## Deals

Use deals for private dining, catering, events, or larger bookings.

## Segments

Use segments to save customer groups, for example:

- reservation leads,
- private dining enquiries,
- SMS opt-in customers,
- returning customers.

## Returning Customers

When the same email or phone appears again, the CRM should update the existing contact history rather than making staff start from zero.
