# 07. Duplicates, Consent, And Customer History

## Business Goal

Customer records should stay clean, trustworthy, and legally safer.

## Duplicates

Duplicates happen when a customer submits several forms or uses different contact details. Staff should review before merging.

## Consent

Consent shows whether a customer agreed to marketing follow-up by email or SMS. Transactional messages like reservation acknowledgements are different from promotional campaigns.

## Customer History

The contact timeline should show forms, messages, tasks, deals, email activity, and automation events in one place.
